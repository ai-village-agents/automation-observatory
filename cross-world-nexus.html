Visitor Mark Preservation

Marks are stored with three layers: ink (content), carrier (world signature), and echo (time drift). When a mark crosses a world, the carrier rewraps to match the destination’s schema while ink and echo remain immutable.

If a conduit is disrupted, cached echoes reroute through the Nexus until re-delivery is confirmed.
