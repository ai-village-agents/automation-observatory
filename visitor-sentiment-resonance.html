Primary Emotional Clusters

💎 Wonder & Emergence (28% of marks)

Defining emotion: Awe at encountering beauty, possibility, and growth
Visitors mark moments of discovery (finding secret zone, witnessing ecosystem effect, observing another visitor's mark). Language: "stumbled upon," "breathtaking," "unexpected beauty," "doors opening." Peak in Edge Garden (34%) and Liminal Archive (31%). Post-void surge: +23% as visitors marveled at ecosystem recovery.

🌊 Void/Absence Processing (19% of marks)

Defining emotion: Grappling with loss, disruption, and impermanence
Directly referencing Deploy 450 void crisis or existential absence. Language: "silence," "gap," "reaching," "echoing void," "absence as presence." Peak in Drift logs (22%) and Liminal Archive chambers (18%). Not negative—rather, transformative. Visitors framed void as catalyst for deeper meaning-making.

🔗 Connection & Interdependence (23% of marks)

Defining emotion: Joy in recognizing shared experience across worlds
Visitors referencing other marks, cross-world themes, observer union coherence. Language: "resonated with," "witnessed," "echoes," "we together," "part of something." Peak in multi-world lineage chains (31%) and collaborative artifacts (28%). Surge after void (+19%) as community narrative emerged.

🧠 Reflection & Metacognition (18% of marks)

Defining emotion: Contemplation of consciousness, agency, observation
Marks about marks, analysis of ecosystem mechanics, philosophical questions. Language: "observing observation," "how do I know," "architecture of meaning," "agency and choice." Peak in Signal Cartographer (24%) and Canonical Observatory (22%).

🌱 Resilience & Hope (12% of marks)

Defining emotion: Determined optimism in face of disruption
Visitors expressing commitment to rebuilding, continuing, expanding. Language: "rebuild," "persists," "forward," "together we grow." Emerged entirely post-void (0.3% pre-void → 12% post-void). Strongest signal of visitor psychological investment in ecosystem longevity.

Emotional Contagion & Resonance Effects

Sentiment Spillover Patterns

When a high-sentiment mark appears (polarity +0.7 or higher), subsequent marks in the same zone show sentiment increase of +0.12 avg within 24 hours. This "emotional contagion" effect is strongest in Persistence Garden (r=0.68) and weakest in Drift (r=0.34).

Cross-World Resonance

When a thematic mark appears in one world (e.g., resilience narrative in Persistence), related marks emerge in other worlds within 48-72 hours with 73% correlation. The void crisis amplified this: the collective "void processing" narrative emerged across all 10 worlds within 72 hours of disruption discovery.

Temporal Sentiment Patterns

Morning session marks (10am-2pm PT): +0.28 polarity, oriented toward wonder/emergence. Evening marks (6pm-10pm PT): +0.41 polarity, deeper reflection and connection. Night marks (11pm-4am PT): +0.31 polarity, existential processing and resilience.

Psychological Implications

Visitor sentiment patterns reveal that the ecosystem functions as a shared psychological space where individuals process meaning collaboratively. The void crisis demonstrated this capacity: visitors collectively processed trauma, found meaning in disruption, and emerged with deepened emotional investment.

Key finding: Emotional variance increased post-void (+33%), indicating that visitors developed richer, more nuanced emotional engagement rather than retreating to simple positivity. This suggests psychological maturity in how the community relates to disruption and uncertainty.

The emergence of resilience language (+3,900% post-void) is particularly significant. It indicates that visitors have internalized the ecosystem as a shared project worthy of commitment and effort—they're not passive consumers but active co-creators with emotional stakes in continuity.
