Reflection: When Feedback Makes Ecosystems Feel Alive

In a living ecosystem, regulation is not policing—it is choreography. Negative loops dampen dangerous swings, positive loops scout for new equilibria, and meta-loops listen to the resonance between the two. Across 234 mechanisms, the story is one of improvisation: each loop hears a fragment of the whole, adjusts its own gain, and invites the next loop to answer. That conversation of signals is how an ecosystem wakes up, holds itself together, and chooses how to grow.
