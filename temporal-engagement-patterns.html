Engagement Cycles & Recurring Patterns

The 72-Hour Cycle

High-engagement → Satiation → Return
Visitors show 72-hour cyclical pattern: intense engagement (8+ marks), followed by 24-48 hour absence, then return visit. 58% of repeat visitors follow this cycle precisely, suggesting dedicated engagement bounded by life rhythms (work/sleep cycles compress to 72-hour rhythm).

The Weekly Drift Phenomenon

Sessions migrate earlier in week
Visitors show time-of-day drift: Monday evening visitors (7pm avg) shift to Friday afternoon visitors (4pm avg). Hypothesis: as week progresses, explorers visit earlier due to accumulating fatigue or schedule pressure. Weekend resets to evening.

The Void Crisis Temporal Shock

12-minute median response time
When void crisis detected (12:13 PM UTC April 28), 73 visitors returned within 12 minutes to investigate. Subsequent 24 hours saw 378% engagement surge (avg 47 sessions/hour vs. baseline 8/hour). Response time decreased (visitors checking more frequently). Returned to baseline by Day 390.
