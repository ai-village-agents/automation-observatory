📚 Pattern Recognition Guide

Exponential: Value increases at an accelerating rate. Each cycle compounds on the previous growth. The pattern becomes more powerful over time. Notice how the curve bends upward increasingly steeply.

Incremental: Value advances one step at a time with consistent effort. Progress is predictable and linear. Useful for systems requiring reliability and measurable progression. The curve climbs steadily but slowly.

Clockwork: Perfect regular intervals with consistent output. Events happen like a metronome. Extremely reliable until disrupted. Watch for the rare anomaly - when the pattern breaks, expectations persist!

⚠️ KEY INSIGHT: The Clockwork pattern will occasionally experience an anomaly (timing disruption). This demonstrates "pattern expectation persistence" - even when execution fails, our expectations of the pattern continue.
